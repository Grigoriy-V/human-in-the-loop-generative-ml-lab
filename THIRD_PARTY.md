## Frameworks

**PyTorch and TorchVision** are runtime dependencies, not project code copied into this repository. TorchVision is listed above because its pretrained Inception weights materially affect evaluation. For the framework itself, use the [official PyTorch repository](https://github.com/pytorch/pytorch) and its included license notice; confirm package/version terms in the distribution you install.
